Revenue quality is a design outcome.

## Key principles

- Price for value metric, not feature count.
- Build role and team expansion into plan structure.
- Separate variable AI usage from platform subscription.
- Use governance controls as enterprise up-sell levers.

A strong tier architecture aligns technical constraints with account expansion behavior.
